1. Introduction (3-4 pages)

1.1 Motivation

  • Depression is a major global health challenge
  • 332 million people affected worldwide (WHO)
  • 5.7% of adults globally
  • 1.5x more common in women
  • Leading cause of disability worldwide
  • The treatment gap: Only ~1/3 receive treatment even in wealthy countries
  • Stigma, access barriers, shortage of mental health professionals
  • Diagnosis relies on subjective self-report
  • The need for objective markers
  • Physical conditions have blood tests, imaging, etc.
  • Mental health lacks objective biomarkers
  • Speech may contain measurable indicators
